// ABOUT

Built by operators. Run by autonomous agents.

IronBark Security Solutions was founded as a capstone project by four IST students at Cal State San Bernardino — four people who'd rather ship a working red-team platform than a PowerPoint deck.

Our thesis

Most security firms charge by the hour and deliver a PDF twice a year. That doesn't scale, and it doesn't tell you what's exploitable on your network today. So we built ClaWD — an autonomous red-team agent that runs continuously, reports in real time, and costs a fraction of a retained pen-test team.

Our boundaries

We operate under strict rules of engagement. Every scan is authorized. Every engagement has a signed scope. ClaWD is not a script kiddie — it's a constrained, logged, audited system designed for enterprise use where uptime and trust matter.
